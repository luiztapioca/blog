## Why "Coffee & Tapioca"?

![caf√©-tapioca.png](img/logo.png)

In the "Coffee and Tapioca" philosophy, coffee embodies the restless fervor of the soul, a fever of emotions oscillating between anger and bitter anguish, reflecting the conflict within our being. On the other hand, tapioca emerges as a vital source, delicate and pure, symbolizing the serene acceptance of life amidst chaos; offering joy and pleasure in the journey of existence.
